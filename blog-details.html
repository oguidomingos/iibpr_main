Technetium has opened its first coffee shop under the Di'Anthony franchise in Wolli Creek, NSW

Where's Wolli hosted a very successful family and friends launch event on the 26th of May.

The event included attendance from family and friends of the cafes two owners, Dimitri and Anthony. Technetium provided photography and videography of the event, which successfully promoted the opening of the cafe. Special attendees included known food bloggers to assist with promotion of Where’s Wolli. Their feedback was positive and the success of the opening event set a strong and positive vibe for the first day of trade.

The boys have done an outstanding job with the decor and complete fitting of the cafe. From pipe to plate, Where’s Wolli creates a vibe of many natures. It is a place to relax alone, or laugh with friends. It is a place to enjoy good coffee and food that takes your tastebuds to another dimension. It is a place to grab a coffee and a quick meal before heading off for the day.

With the convenient location of Wolli Creek, a well-known ‘change’ station for those riding our railways as well as being located minutes away from Sydney Airport. Airport staff have described it as a ‘blessing in disguise’ as they are able to grab their hands on a coffee before their early morning shift. Just opposite Cahill Park, the location provides space and scenery for families to enjoy a meal as well as the outdoors.

Di'Anthony franchise coffee shop open in the Wolli Creek, NSW

- Mr Kappatos

We highly recommend visiting Where’s Wolli, located at: Shop 1, 20-24 Levey Steet, Wolli Creek NSW 22205
